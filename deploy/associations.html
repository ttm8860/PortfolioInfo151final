My Professional and Community Associations

Being actively involved in organizations that align with my identity, interests, and career goals has helped me grow both personally and professionally. One community that has had a particularly meaningful impact on my journey is the Drexel African Student Association (DASA).

DASA connects students of African descent and anyone interested in African culture through cultural events, community service, and student support. As an event organizer for DASA, I help foster a sense of belonging on campus and celebrate the richness of African traditions and perspectives.
